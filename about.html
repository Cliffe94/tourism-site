About Finn Safaris

Experience Kenya like never before with Finn Safaris – your trusted travel partner for unforgettable adventures.

Who We Are

Founded in Kenya, Finn Safaris is a locally owned tourism company dedicated to offering the best safari experiences across East Africa. Inspired by top agencies like Mufasa Safaris and Pollmans, our mission is to combine professional service with authentic, memorable journeys.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert local guides with deep knowledge of the wildlife and landscape.
  • Affordable, flexible tour packages tailored to your preferences.
  • Modern, well-maintained safari vehicles for comfort and safety.
  • Personalized customer service with 24/7 support.

Our Vision

To become Kenya’s most trusted name in safari tourism while preserving nature and supporting local communities.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable, and authentic travel experiences across Kenya and beyond.
